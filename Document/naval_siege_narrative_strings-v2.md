## 3. Phase C: Zone Resolution (The Tally)

**Ally Top Deck:**
* **[Secured]** "The last of the boarders is thrown to the sea. The top deck remains ours—stained with blood, but unbroken."
* **[Overrun]** "We've lost the surface! The enemy runs rampant, tearing into our crew and smashing our topside defenses to kindling."

**Ally Cannon Deck:**
* **[Secured]** "The gun crews cheer through the smoke. The cannons are secure, the powder is dry, and we are ready to unleash hell."
* **[Overrun]** "Screams echo from below. Saboteurs have spiked the cannons and slaughtered the crews. Our broadside is severely crippled."

**Ally Rigging:**
* **[Secured]** "High above the chaos, our rigging holds firm. The sails catch the wind, keeping us nimble in the water."
* **[Overrun]** "A massive crack echoes from above. The enemy has severed the main lines. Sails tear, and the ship groans, turning sluggish and vulnerable."

**Enemy Main Deck (Vanguard):**
* **[Secured]** "The Vanguard holds the line! A mountain of enemy dead marks their position, securing the bridgehead for our strike team."
* **[Overrun]** "The Vanguard has broken. The enemy surges forward, hacking away the grappling lines. The strike team is trapped behind enemy lines with no escape!"

**Enemy Interior (Cannon Deck / Armory / Officer Qtrs):**
* **[Secured]** "A muffled explosion shakes the enemy vessel from within. The strike team has successfully crippled their target!"
